What is NJ Cannabis Insider?

With a team of renowned journalists from NJ.com and The Star-Ledger, NJ Cannabis Insider will inform you about the issues, policies, laws, regulations, politics and people who are making an impact on the cannabis industry, community and marketplace.

There are billions of dollars at stake in a new market so lucrative that entrepreneurs everywhere are eyeing New Jersey. It’s an economic opportunity unrivaled in modern state history, but obstacles loom large. NJ Cannabis Insider will help you navigate those obstacles and connect you with others in the industry.
